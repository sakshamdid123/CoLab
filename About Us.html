About CoLab

After noticing that many student-run businesses within Delhi University remained confined to their own colleges, we launched a social initiative aimed at connecting these ventures across the university. Our goal is to break down barriers, providing a platform that exposes student businesses to a broader audience and fosters collaboration between colleges. What sets us apart is our commitment to supporting these entrepreneurs without charging any commission, ensuring that all profits stay with the students. Through events, social media campaigns, and a dedicated platform, we aim to build a more interconnected and thriving student business ecosystem across Delhi University.
